Slow Cooker Chicken Thighs

This recipe is super hands-off. Once you make the dressing, you can't mess it up! The slow cooker makes such tender and flavorful chicken without you standing over it.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon dry sherry
  • 1 & 1/2 teaspoons toasted sesame oil
  • 1 & 1/2 teaspoons grated fresh ginger
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon five-spice powder
  • 3 pounds boneless skinless chicken thighs

Directions

  1. Combine hoisin, ketchup, honey, soy sauce, sherry, sesame oil, grated ginger, garlic, and five-spice powder in a measuring cup. Whisk until combined.
  2. Place chicken in slow cooker and pour half of the sauce mixture over top, turning the chicken to coat evenly. Refrigerate remaining sauce.
  3. Cover the cooker and put on low heat for five hours.
  4. Remove chicken from slow cooker and placed on a foil lined baking sheet. Brush remaining sauce over top of the chicken. Put chicken in broiler for three minutes until brown. Remove from broiler and serve immediately
